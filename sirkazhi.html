Thiru Kazhiseerama Vinnagaram
Sri TriVikraman

TriVikrama Perumal-(file picture)

This is one of the 108 divya desams in Tamil Nadu. This temple is very near Sirkazhi railway station.

As per legends, Lord Brahmma grew proud of his boon of longevity spanning many Yugas.Lord Narayana wanted to teach him a lesson. Sage Romesa performed penance here for the darshan of Lord. Lord appeared before the sage and said that he would live longer than Brahmma whose longevity would be reduced by a year when one hair fell from his body. Brahmma repented his mistake for being proud and arrogant about his longevity that Lord had indirectly taught him a lesson. Lord Narayana graced this place with His Trivikrama form,scaling the whole world with left foot pointing toward the sky.

This is where Thirumangai Azhwar had a competetion with Sambandar and sang 10 verses describing the ten incarnations of Lord Vishnu. ThiruGnana Sambandar was very happy and rewarded Azhwar his Vel (spear) and an anklet. This incident also showed the the unity of Saivam and Vaishnavam.

Moolavar of this sthalam is  TriVikraman,in Nindra Thirukkolam (Standing posture) facing east.

Prathyaksham for Ashtakona Maharishi.

Thayaar in this sthalam is Loganayaki.

Pushkarani(holy tank) of this place is Sangapushkarini,Chakratheertham.

Mangalasasanams The temple is revered by the verses of Manavaala Munigal

Temple location It is situated in the town of Sirkazhi, very near to Sirkazhi railway station.

Temple Timings It is normally open from 7 am – 11:30 am and 4 pm-8 pm.

How to reach?
It is situated around 10 kms from Sirkazhi. There are buses from Mayavaram/ Sirkazhi that goes past Thiru Nangur. Direct special buses are available only on special occassions.If you drive your car it is 20 kms from Mayavaram and 10 kms from Sirkazhi. The road leading to this village is narrow and you might miss the turning from the main road if you are not watchful.

Where to Stay?There are good hotels in Sirkazhi. Mayavaram nearby also has good hotels to stay
Where to Eat?There are several decent restaurants here where you can have good food through the day.
Places nearby Mayavaram,ThiruNangur (11 divya desams),Vaitheeswaran koil, Navagraha sthalangal - Thiruvengadu,Keezhperum pallam.
