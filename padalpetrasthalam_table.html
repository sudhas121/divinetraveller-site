Padal Petra Siva Sthalams

The Paadal Petra Thalam are 275 temples of Lord Shiva that are revered in the verses of Saiva Nayanmars Thirunavukarasar, Sambandar and Sundarar who lived between 7th and 9th century A.D. These Shiva Temples have been in existence for more than 1000 years Majority of the temples are in Tamil Nadu. There are 265 temples in Tamil Nadu, 2 in Andhra pradesh, 1 in kerala, 1 in karnataka, 2 temples in Uttarakhand, 2 temples in Sri Lanka, 1 temple in Nepal, and Tirukayilaayam in Mount Kailash.
