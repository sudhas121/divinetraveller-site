Kainkaryam:: To give Oil to Shiva temples

A Siva lingam in one of the temples

There are lot of Siva temples especially, Thevaram Padal Petra Sthalangal (274 in number ) are very importanat to Hindus. But unfortunately, these PPS are in such a pathetic and very poor financial condition that there is no money even to buy oil to light the Lamps nor to buy flowers for the deities. Once during a visit to a remote Sivan temple Mahaperiva was distressed to see that the temple lacked funds even to light a lamp. He desired that the devotees should come forward by some means to contribute for oil to light the 'deepams' in the Shivalayas and no temple should be without lights. With the blessings and intention of fulfilling at least a fraction of Sri Sri Mahaperiva's wishes, the following bagavat Kainkaryam has been intiated by a small team of like minded devotees.

This kainkaryam is to give oil in tins (Each 15 ltrs.), to the shiva temples, on a monthly basis, to light the lamps in the temples.

On a monthly basis, we give oil tins to 60 to 70 Shiva temples spread throughout Tamil Nadu.

Almost all Padal Petra Sthalam (PPS) are in bad condition except those within the city. The temple Kurukals are not paid salary regularly. Hence being an Hindu it is our main duty to support these temples in some way or the other. The very basic support we can extend to these temples is to give oil to light the lamps. Undersigned (Adiyen) is doing this kainkaryam of supplying oil tins of each 15 litres to Padal Petra Sthalangals (PPS)for the past six years



Each oil tin costs around Rs. 2200.
This kainkaryam has been started with Maha Periva blessings. As on date there are about 100 plus devotees from India and abroad participating in this kainkaryam.

Hinduism Today, a quarterly published in the United States of America has higlighted our service in their article 'Keeping the Lamps Lit' refer: Hinduism Today article


The kainkaryam works as below:
The devotees send their contribution to the below mentioned bank account. A few meet us in person and donate cash. With this money collected, the team purchases Oil tins, from the oil shops,spread throughout Tamil Nadu This is “Yatha Sakthi” (as far as strength allows /as far as possible) contribution. We receive contribution right from Rs. 100 to Rs.1500 per month. I have tied with big oil shops in major towns like Thanjavur,Kumbakonam,Thiruvarur,Thivaiyaru,Tirunelveli,Trichy,Mayavaram, Sirkazhi, Kancheepuram. Every month I send money to these shops through NEFT. I have hired Auto Rickshaw drivers in these towns.These auto drivers go to the respective oil shops, collect the oil tins and deliver it to the temples. I strongly believe that it the prime most responsibility of we Hindus is to support these sivan koils by giving "yatha sakthi" contribution. There are lots and lots of shiva temples, which need oil. So I request the devotees to support me in this holy kainkaryam, so that we can serve more and more temples.A small monthly contribution will go a long way. Not restricting ourselves to Shivalayas alone we also go to Sri Narayana temples.We give one tin of ghee ( 15 KG) to Lakshmi Narasimhar temple at Narasimhapuram in Poondamallee – Arakonam Road and Ulagalanda Perumal at Thirukovilur.
