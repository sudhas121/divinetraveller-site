Pandit Sangenthi Natesa Sastri (1859 – 1906)

           My great grand father Natesa Sastri,was born in the year 1859, to Shri Mahalinga Iyer and Smt Akilandeshwari, in village Sangendhi, near Lalgudi, Trichy District, Tamil Nadu. He had his early education in Kumbakonam and later in Madras University and received his B.A.Degree. In the year 1881 he was employed in Department of Art and Sculpture, Indian Government, under British Officer Robert Sewell (1845–1925). His salary was Rs.30/- per month. Robert Sewell was impressed by his knowledge and command in Sanskrit, he revised his salary to Rs.75/- per month and honored him with title “Pandit”.
            Pandit Sastri knew 18 languages. He had absolute command in Tamil, Sanskrit and English. He was interested and researched on Vedas, Upanishads, Indian religion,festival and culture of South India. He wrote a number of books in Tamil, Sanskrit and English. He not only translated some English and Sanskrit works but also penned several books.
            Some of his works are: Valmiki Ramayanam, Dinadayalu, Dikkatra Iru Kuzandaigal, Thakkanathu Poorva Kadaigal, Thakkanathu Madya Kadaigal, Drivida Natu Kadaigal, Madhiketta Manaivi, Thalayanai Madiram, Mami Goluvirukkai, Thooku Thooki (made into a popular motion picture with Sivaji Ganesan in the lead) in Tamil.
            After finishing Valmiki Ramayanam in Tamil, he wanted U. V. Swaminatha Iyer ( who became very famous later) to correct his works (not proof read) for him. At that time U.Ve Sa was busy in publishing old tamil literature,yet he corrected certain parts of Valmiki Ramayanam for his friens Sastri.
            In Sanskrit he wrote, Kundala Nataka Vimarisanam, Raghuvamsam, Harsha Charitra Vimarisanam.
            Many of his English works are still popular even now such as Hindu Fasts & Feasts, South Indian Plays, Dravida Night Folk Stories, King and his Four Ministers, Sun Stories, Tenaliraman stories. Some of his english works are available in many US libraries and libraries in UK, and few are available online through Amazon,Flipkart.
